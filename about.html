About the server

The Rules

Rule #1 - Keep the server a positive and inclusive place.

Rule #2 - Use only English unless in a language lobby.

Rule #3 - No spam or crossposting.

Rule #4 - No sharing Discord servers or roleplay (RP) requests.

Rule #5 - No explicit or suggestive content of any kind.

Rule #6 - No politically or morally sensitive content.

Rule #7 - Respect intellectual property.

Rule #8 - Read the server's rule page to receive the entry code.

Rule #9 - Have fun!

The roles

Newts: When you first join the server, you'll be a newt, which is a pun on "new". You'll have access to a newt-specific channel to speak with other newts.

Premium members: Once you've been in the server for a month and have more than 1500 messages, you'll become a premium member.

Advanced micronations: If you are very active in the server you'll become an advanced micronation.

Taxpayers: This is our adult role. It requires manual assignment by a mod.

Regional roles: These are self-assignable roles that indicate which continent you're from.

Language roles: These are self-assignable roles that will give you access to the language lobbies. Only take them if you will speak the language.

Game roles: These are self-assignable, pingable roles for playing various group videogames in this server. Only take them if you're willing to be pinged.

Hetagamers: This is a self-assignable role that gives you access to the Hetagames channel to discuss Hetalia games, i.e. Hetaoni and Dreamtalia.

Pingable roles: These are self-assignable roles that mods ping for events or for occasional check-ins.

Notification preferences: These are self-assignable roles to show others whether you are comfortable with being @ pinged.

Misc. channels: These are self-assignable roles that give you access to additional channels in the server.

Pronoun roles: These are self-assignable roles that can quickly let others know your preferred pronouns.

The channels

Rules and announcements: Rules, announcements, FAQ, self-assignable roles, and intros.

Promos: Promotion channel. Conversations here are not allowed and you may only promote the same thing once a month.

Chatz: Contains the main conversation lobbies, as well as positivity and venting channels.

Old folks home: Adult channels. You will need a mod to assign you the "taxpayer" role to access these.

Hetalia: Channels for Hetalia-related conversations. We have aph, headcanons, au and OCs channels.

Hetavision: Channels for the discussion of the annual Eurovision Song Contest.

Creative corner: All things creative go here. We have channels for WIPs, finished art, cosplay and fanfics.

Community center: Channels for that allow you to get to know your fellow server members!

Misc. Media: Channels for you to share media with other members.

Language lobbies: We have 10 language channels where you'll be able to speak other languages.

Voice chat: You can play music, stream, or play videogames with other Hetalia fans in our voice channels!

Chillzone: Channels to isolate and speak to members directly regarding rule violations.
