Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

Click a "join" button anywhere on this website to join our Discord server. You will arrive in the server's landing page. The rest of the server is locked until you read the rules. We have strict rules about sensitive content, so it's important you read everything fully. You may have a 10-minute waiting period on your account after joining; please use this time to read the rules. If you're still confused on how to enter the server after reading the rules, please DM @Pierre#9707 to contact server moderators. See you soon!

Why should I fill out an introduction?

It is helpful for us to know things like what to call you, your pronouns, and your age to verify your eligibility for the adult role. It also indicates you are active in the community and allows us to know more about you and start conversations based on your intro!

I have an issue with a mod, another user, or the way the server is being run.

You have the option of (1) DMing @Pierre#9707 to submit your concerns to the mod team anonymously or (2) DMing an uninvolved admin with your concern, and they will protect your anonymity. Please provide screenshots and any necessary information for your concern, and we will investigate it right away.

How can I promote the server?

You can help by retweeting our Pinned Tweet (@hetaliadiscord), our Tumblr post (@hetaliadiscord), Instagram post (@hetaliadiscord), or sharing our website's link- http://hetalia.net/. We also have YouTube and TikTok!

Where can I leave feedback for the server?

Please DM any feedback to @PierreBot#9707 on Discord!

Are you planning movie nights, game nights, or other events?

We are in need of event planners who can help us coordinate more events. If you'd like to help, please DM @Pierrebot#9707 and tell us who you are!

What do Boosters of the server get?

Boosters of the server get the Blooster role, a custom color role, 4 emoji slots per boost depending on availability, and access to the newts club!

What are the ranked roles and how are mods chosen?

Everyone starts as a newt, and then receive premium members role if they are active for around a month. Bloosters are those who boost the server!

Mods are currently appointed through closed auditions, but we may open up mod applications in the future.

Are learners of a language allowed to join language lobbies?

Yes! We ask that you try to limit English conversation as much as possible, especially when not related to learning the language, in these lobbies though to keep it a place where both learners and native speakers can interact freely.

What language lobbies are currently available?

French (Français), Spanish (Español), German (Deutsch), Italian (Italiano), Malay (Melayu, Bahasa Indonesia), Filipino (Tagalog, Bisaya, Kapampangan), Japanese (日本語), Portuguese (Português), Skandanavian (Norsk, Svenska, Dansk) and Chinese (中文, 廣東話).

Who is Pierrebot and what does he do?

Pierrebot is the server's modmail bot. Whatever you send to him will be seen by the whole mod team, and it is completely anonymous. Please only contact Pierrebot for serious questions and problems.

How old do I have to be to request access to the adult channels?

You need to be 18+ regardless of your country's age of majority. For adults looking to access the 18+ channels you need to indicate you're above 18 in your intro to acknowledge that you're an adult, then message @Pierrebot#9707 for the role. Please note: lying about your age to access adult channels will result in an instant ban from the server and we will report your account to Discord.
