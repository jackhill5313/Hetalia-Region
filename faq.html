Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

First, click the link to join. Then, read the rules.

Why should I fill out an introduction?

It's extremely helpful for us to know things like what to call you, your pronouns, and your age for purposes of our minor and adult roles, which are in place for safety purposes. It also just helps us get to know more about you and start conversations based on what you put into it!

I have an issue with the way the server's being run/a mod/Joe/another user.

PM us, and we'll get to investigating it right away! Feel free to provide screenshots or anything else you might feel necessary. Feel free to use an alt if you want to remain anonymous.

How can I promote the server?

We'd love it if you did, the more Hetalia fans here, the better! You can help by retweeting our Pinned Tweet on our Twitter (@hetaliadiscord), our Tumblr post on our Tumblr, (@hetaliadiscord), Instagram post (@hetaliadiscord), or simply spreading links to our website- http://hetalia.net/ ! We also have a Tiktok and YouTube account, so if you ever have ideas for videos, let us know!

Where can I leave feedback on the server?

In our feedback survey, which we highly encourage to fill out so we can improve the server!

Are you planning movie nights/game nights/events?

Yes! But we'd love your help! If you have any specific suggestions for movies, games, or events, we'd love to hear them!

What do Boosters of the server get?

Boosters of the server get 4 emoji per boost added to the server, the Blooster role, a custom color role, and possibly more perks to come, such as exclusive giveaways of Hetalia merch!

What are the ranked roles/how are mods chosen?

Everyone starts out as a newt! Once you are active within the server and don't get a rules strike for, usually, a month or two, you go up to a premium member! The time for this to happen, however, lengthens if you happen to get a strike/go inactive. Bloosters are those who boost the server!

To get the "advanced micronations" role, you must be active within the server, and this is calculated by an average of your messages sent per day!

Mods are appointed through careful review of mod applications and are based off of activity, helpfulness/kindness/friendliness within the server, and understanding of the rules.

Are learners of a language allowed to join language lobbies?

Yes! We ask that you try to limit English conversation as much as possible, especially when not related to learning the language, in these lobbies though to keep it a place where both learners and native speakers can interact freely.

What language lobbies are currently available?

French (Français), Spanish (Español), Italian (Italiano), Malay, Tagalog/Taglish, Japanese (日本語), and Chinese (中文).

Who is Pierrebot and what does he do?

Pierrebot is the server's modmail bot! Whatever you send to him will be seen by the whole mod team, and it is completely anonymous. Please only contact Pierrebot for serious questions and problems!

How old do I have to be to gain entry to the adult chat/server?

18.
